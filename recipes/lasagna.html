Odin's Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious Italian pasta dish known for its layers of wide noodles, ricotta and mozzarella cheeses, and a hearty tomato-based sauce, all baked to perfection. Its combination of savory flavors and creamy textures makes it a beloved comfort food enjoyed by many.
Ingredients:
For the Meat Sauce:
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or ground Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (12 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the Cheese Filling:
  • 15 ounces (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups (200g) chredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Additional Ingredients:
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese (for topping)
  • Fresh basil or parsley leaves for garnish (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet or saucepan, brown the ground beef or sausage over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain excess fat.
    • Add copped onion and minced garlic to the meat and saute until the onion is translucent.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce.
    • Season with dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce on low heat for about 30 mnutes, stirring occasionaly.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Filling:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan Cheese, fresh parsley (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 350 F (190C).
    • In a 9x13 (23x33cm) baking dish, spread a small amount of meat sauce on the bottom to prevent sticking.
    • LAyer with 4 cooked lasagna noodles.
    • Add half of the cheese filling and spread it evenly over the noodles.
    • Top with a layer of meat sauce.
    • Repeat the layers: noodles, remaining cheese filling, meat sace.
    • Finish with a final layer of 4 noodles and a generous topping of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, tented so it doesn't touch the cheese.
    • Bake for 25 minutes,
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is golden brown.
  5. Rest and Serve:
    • Allow the lasagna to rest for about 10 minutes before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley leaves if desired.
Enjoy your homemade lasagna with a side salad and some garlic bread for a delicious meal!
